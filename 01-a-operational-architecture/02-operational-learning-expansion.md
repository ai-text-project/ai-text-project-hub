# Operational Learning Expansion

**Architecture Version:** v1.0 (Provisional)

---

# Purpose

This document records an operational observation concerning the internal expansion of the Research Program Operational Cycle.

Whereas the Operational Cycle defines the high-level operational architecture of the Research Program, repeated operational practice has revealed that some stages of the cycle contain additional internal learning structures that were not explicitly represented in the original architecture.

The purpose of this document is to preserve these observations without modifying the Operational Cycle itself.

---

# Background

The Research Program Operational Cycle provides a stable operational framework describing how research continuously evolves through communication, observation, reflection, comparative review, and operational learning.

The architecture intentionally represents these activities at a high level.

However, practical operation during the Presentation Development Project revealed that the internal learning process contains additional organizational layers.

---

# Observed Operational Expansion

The original Operational Cycle represents the following sequence.

```text
Research Assets

↓

Communication Assets

↓

Audience Dialogue

↓

Observation

↓

Reflection

↓

Comparative Review

↓

Research Program Evolution
```

During practical operation, the Reflection and Comparative Review stages gradually expanded into a more detailed operational learning process.

The observed operational sequence became:

```text
Observation

↓

Asset Inventory

↓

Asset Classification

↓

Asset Relationship Map

↓

Asset Evolution

↓

Strategic Interpretation

↓

Comparative Review
```

The additional stages emerged naturally through repeated operational review rather than through prior architectural design.

---

# Interpretation

This observation does not indicate a change in the Operational Architecture.

Instead, it suggests that the Reflection and Comparative Review phases contain multiple internal learning activities that become visible only through sustained operational practice.

The original Operational Cycle therefore remains valid as the high-level operational architecture.

The expanded sequence represents an operational refinement observed during practical implementation.

---

# Relationship to the Operational Cycle

The relationship between the original Operational Cycle and the expanded learning process may be interpreted as follows.

```text
Research Assets

↓

Communication Assets

↓

Audience Dialogue

↓

Observation

↓

Reflection
      │
      ├── Asset Inventory
      ├── Asset Classification
      ├── Asset Relationship Map
      ├── Asset Evolution
      └── Strategic Interpretation

↓

Comparative Review

↓

Research Program Evolution
```

The internal expansion provides a higher-resolution view of operational learning while preserving the original architectural structure.

---

# Operational Significance

The observation suggests that completed research projects may continue generating reusable research assets after their primary objectives have been achieved.

Operational learning therefore extends beyond project completion.

Instead, completed projects become inputs for additional organizational learning, methodological refinement, and architectural understanding.

---

# Current Interpretation

At the present stage, this observation should be regarded as an operational refinement of the Research Program Operational Cycle.

The observation does not replace the existing architecture.

Instead, it provides a more detailed operational perspective that may support future refinement of operational methodologies and Harvest processes.

Further observations across additional projects will be required to determine whether this expanded learning structure consistently reappears within the Research Program.
