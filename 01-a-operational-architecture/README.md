# Operational Architecture

This directory documents the **Operational Architecture** of the Research Program.

While the **Research Architecture** describes the structural organization of the research space, the **Operational Architecture** explains how research activities continuously generate new observations, communication assets, and methodological evolution through repeated operational learning.

Rather than focusing on individual research methodologies, the documents in this directory describe the operational continuity that enables the Research Program to develop, adapt, and evolve over time.

The primary objective is to clarify how multiple research activities are connected into a continuous operational cycle that supports sustainable long-term research development.
