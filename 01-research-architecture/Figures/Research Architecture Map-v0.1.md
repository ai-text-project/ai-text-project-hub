
# Research Architecture Map v0.1

## Visual Specification

### Purpose

To visualize the relationship between:

* Dialogue
* Observed Phenomena
* Cases
* Comparative Observation
* Meta Cases
* Research-Space Evolution

while clarifying the position of Meta Cases within the overall research architecture.

---

# Design Principle

This figure is NOT:

* a theory diagram
* a governance diagram
* a repository diagram

This figure IS:

* an orientation map
* a research architecture map
* a positioning diagram

---
