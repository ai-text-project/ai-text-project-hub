# Presentation Harvest Map

**Version:** v1.0 (Frozen Baseline)

---

# Purpose

This document provides an overview of the Presentation Harvest Series.

Rather than describing individual research assets, this document explains how the Harvest documents are organized and how they collectively support the long-term understanding of the Presentation Development Project.

The Harvest Series documents the Presentation Project from multiple complementary perspectives, progressing from asset identification to strategic interpretation.

---

# Document Structure

## 00 — Presentation Assets Inventory

Purpose

Identify and record the research assets generated during the Presentation Development Project.

Primary Perspective

"What was created?"

---

## 01 — Presentation Asset Classification

Purpose

Organize the generated assets according to their operational functions.

Primary Perspective

"What capabilities were formed?"

---

## 02 — Presentation Asset Relationship Map

Purpose

Describe the structural relationships among the generated assets.

Primary Perspective

"How are the assets connected?"

---

## 03 — Presentation Asset Evolution

Purpose

Describe how the Presentation Project progressively evolved into an operational capability.

Primary Perspective

"How did the assets develop?"

---

## 04 — Presentation Strategic Interpretation

Purpose

Interpret the overall strategic significance of the completed Presentation Project.

Primary Perspective

"Why does this project matter within the Research Program?"

# Developmental Flow

The Harvest Series follows a progressive analytical structure.

```text
Identification

↓

Organization

↓

Structural Understanding

↓

Developmental Understanding

↓

Strategic Understanding
```

Each document builds upon the previous one while introducing a broader level of interpretation.

---

# Position within the Research Program

The Presentation Harvest Series is not part of the Presentation itself.

Instead, it represents a secondary research layer that documents, analyzes, and interprets the research assets generated through the Presentation Development Project.

The Harvest Series therefore contributes to the continuous accumulation of reusable knowledge within the Research Program.

---

# Relationship to Other Research Assets

The Harvest Series complements other documentation associated with the Presentation Development Project, including:

- Presentation Framework
- Demonstration Design Specifications
- Dialogue Assets
- Operational Reports
- Research Architecture
- Operational Cycle

Rather than replacing these materials, the Harvest Series provides an integrated perspective across them.

---

# Future Perspective

The structure established by the Presentation Harvest Series may also be applicable to future projects within the Research Program.

Potential examples include:

- Paper Assembly Harvest
- Fear Research Harvest
- Enterprise Observation Harvest
- Research Architecture Harvest

This document therefore serves not only as a navigation map for the Presentation Project but also as a reference model for future Harvest documentation.
