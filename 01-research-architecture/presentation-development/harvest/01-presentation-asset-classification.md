# Presentation Asset Classification

Version: v1.0 (Frozen Baseline)

---

# Purpose

This document classifies the research assets generated during the Presentation Development Project according to their operational function within the Research Program.

Unlike the Asset Inventory, which records what was produced, this document explains the functional role of each asset category and how the overall capability of the Presentation Project was formed.

---

# Classification Principles

Assets are classified according to the role they perform rather than the order in which they were created.

The objective is to reveal the operational capability that emerged from the Presentation Development Project.
