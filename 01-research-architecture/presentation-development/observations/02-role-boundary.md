# Role Boundary in Presentation Development

## Purpose

This document records the observed development of Role Boundary during the completion of Mini Demonstration – Phase B.

Its purpose is not to define a universal organizational framework.

Instead, it preserves the operational role structure that gradually emerged through repeated Presentation Development cycles.

Accordingly, this document should be regarded as an observational record rather than a formal methodology.

---

# Background

At the beginning of Phase B, roles were primarily understood as task assignments.

Each role contributed to a different aspect of Demonstration development.

However, the operational significance of these roles was not yet fully recognized.

Role differentiation initially served practical coordination rather than methodological quality preservation.

---

# Initial Role Recognition

Early development distinguished roles according to immediate responsibilities.

Typical examples included:

* Research Architecture
* Harvest Strategist
* Virtual Architect
* Presentation Methodology Reviewer

At this stage, the primary objective was efficient collaboration during Demonstration development.

The relationships among these roles remained largely procedural.

---

# Observed Development

As repeated development cycles accumulated, the role structure gradually stabilized.

Rather than producing competing interpretations, each role contributed complementary organizational refinement.

Role separation therefore evolved beyond task allocation.

It became an operational mechanism for preserving architectural consistency throughout Presentation Development.

---

# Mature Role Structure

By the completion of Phase B, the role structure had become substantially clearer.

Research Architecture

Preserves the conceptual foundation of the Research Program.

Presentation Methodology

Preserves the developmental process through which Demonstrations are produced.

Harvest Strategist

Evaluates audience relevance, long-term value, and reusable organizational assets.

Presentation Methodology Reviewer

Evaluates architectural consistency, methodological maturity, and Audience Journey.

Virtual Architect

Translates Research Architecture into coherent visual learning experiences while preserving structural integrity.

Each role contributes independently while remaining aligned with the same underlying Research Architecture.

---

# Operational Significance

The observed role structure demonstrates that Role Boundary functions as more than organizational separation.

Repeated observations suggest that clear Role Boundaries contribute to:

* preservation of architectural consistency
* reduction of local optimization
* progressive methodological refinement
* stable quality assurance
* accumulation of reusable organizational assets

Accordingly, Role Boundary gradually emerged as an operational quality mechanism throughout Phase B.

---

# Relationship to Presentation Development Methodology

Presentation Development Methodology describes how Demonstration Packages are progressively developed.

Role Boundary explains how that developmental process remains structurally stable.

These two organizational assets therefore function together.

Role Boundary should therefore be understood as supporting the operational integrity of Presentation Development rather than existing independently.

---

# Observational Characteristics

Several recurring characteristics became apparent during Phase B.

* Role differentiation progressively stabilized.
* Roles refined the same Research Architecture from complementary viewpoints.
* Reviewer observations increasingly focused on methodology rather than individual slides.
* Virtual Architect gradually shifted from visual production toward audience learning design.
* Harvest Strategist increasingly evaluated long-term organizational value rather than presentation quality alone.
* Role Boundary itself became an observable object of methodological reflection.

These observations are recorded without further theoretical interpretation.

---

# Future Validation

The observations recorded here are derived from one completed Demonstration Package.

Future Demonstration Packages will provide opportunities to evaluate whether similar Role Boundary structures naturally emerge under different presentation themes.

Particular attention should be given to whether Role Boundary continues to function as an operational quality mechanism across future Demonstration development.

---

# Position within the Research Architecture

Role Boundary is positioned within the Presentation Development observations as an organizational observation supporting Demonstration development.

It does not prescribe organizational design.

Instead, it preserves the observed relationship between role differentiation and methodological stability that emerged during Phase B.

Accordingly, this document should be regarded as an observational Research Architecture asset rather than a normative organizational framework.
