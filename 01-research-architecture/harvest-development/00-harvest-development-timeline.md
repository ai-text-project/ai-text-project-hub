# Harvest Development Timeline

## Purpose

This document records the developmental history of Harvest Development.

Rather than documenting project milestones, it records how the methodology for connecting the Research Program with society gradually emerged through iterative design, comparison, review, and refinement.

The objective is to preserve the architectural evolution of the Harvest layer as an independent research asset.

---

# Development Timeline

## Phase 1 — Harvest Strategy Formation

### Initial Objective

Establish an operational strategy for introducing the Dialogue-Phase Reasoning Research Program to external organizations.

Primary focus:

- Social connection
- Enterprise communication
- Long-term collaboration
- Research accessibility
