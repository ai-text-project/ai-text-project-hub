# Meeting Preparation Package v1.0

This package supports operational preparation for Enterprise Comparative Dialogue.

The purpose is not to redesign the Observation Kit, but to prepare reusable operational documents for each enterprise meeting.

---

## Package Contents

1. Meeting Memo

2. Observation Memo

3. Reflection Memo

These three documents are prepared before every enterprise meeting and updated immediately after the meeting.

---

## Operational Principle

Observation is performed during the meeting.

Reflection is performed after the meeting.

The outputs become operational records for future comparative review.
