# Pre-Release Quality Gate

(## Task Report for Harvest Strategist v2 (Provisional))


## Subject

Establishment of a Pre-Release Quality Review Process for the Enterprise Presentation Package

---

# Background

As the Enterprise Presentation Package approached its first external deployment, an independent review process was introduced prior to initiating Observation001.

The objective was not to redesign the Research Program, Presentation Architecture, or Repository Structure.

Instead, the review focused on determining whether the package had reached a stable release quality suitable for initial enterprise observation.

This role was intentionally positioned as a temporary Pre-Release Quality Gate.

---

# Review Scope

The review explicitly excluded:

- Research methodology
- Presentation architecture
- Repository architecture
- Enterprise strategy

The review focused exclusively on release readiness from the perspective of an enterprise audience.

---

# Review Artifacts

Four review deliverables were completed.

## 1. Repository Navigation Review

Evaluation of repository entry structure and navigation flow.

Result:

- Freeze

---

## 2. Enterprise User Journey Review

Evaluation of how a first-time enterprise visitor experiences the package.

Result:

- Freeze

---

## 3. Structural Projection Report

Evaluation of consistency between repository architecture and publicly projected information structure.

Result:

- Freeze

---

## 4. Minor Navigation Improvements

Review of small refinements prior to release.

Findings consisted exclusively of optional usability improvements.

No structural redesign was recommended.

Result:

- Freeze

---

# Overall Assessment

The review identified no major issues requiring redesign.

No changes were recommended for:

- Repository hierarchy
- Presentation sequence
- Research architecture
- Information architecture

The remaining suggestions were limited to minor usability refinements such as reading guidance and navigation assistance.

These items were judged to be non-blocking for Observation001.

---

# Operational Observation

An unexpected finding emerged during the review.

Initially, the role appeared to be responsible for reviewing individual documents.

However, the actual review target evolved into the overall communication environment.

The review therefore shifted from document quality toward experience quality.

The package was ultimately evaluated as an integrated audience experience rather than a collection of independent assets.

---

# Role Boundary Confirmation

The review confirmed that the Enterprise Presentation Package Reviewer should not function as a design role.

Instead, its primary responsibility is to operate as an independent Quality Gate immediately before external release.

Its mission is to determine:

> "Is the package ready for public observation?"

rather than

> "How should the package be redesigned?"

This distinction remained stable throughout the review process.

---

# Recommendation for Harvest Strategist v2

The completed review process should be regarded as a reusable operational asset.

Future enterprise campaigns may reuse the same Quality Gate immediately before external deployment.

However, the role itself does not need to operate continuously.

Instead, it should be activated only when a presentation package approaches release.
