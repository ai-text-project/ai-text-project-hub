# Paper Assembly

## Purpose

This directory preserves the architectural development of the Paper Assembly process observed during the first complete Stage 2 (20-Crystallization) manuscript project.

Its purpose is not to provide an editorial manual.

Instead, it documents how a reproducible Paper Assembly process gradually emerged through observation, interpretation, architectural organization, and methodological externalization.

Accordingly, the documents contained in this directory should be regarded as Research Architecture assets rather than project-specific manuscript resources.

---

The documents follow the observed developmental sequence through which the Paper Assembly process became progressively organized.

# Position within the Research Architecture

Paper Assembly is positioned within the Research Architecture as a reusable organizational layer supporting scholarly manuscript development.

Unlike individual research papers, these documents describe the operational architecture through which observational evidence may be progressively transformed into coherent scholarly manuscripts.

Accordingly, the directory belongs to the Research Architecture layer rather than to any specific research project.
