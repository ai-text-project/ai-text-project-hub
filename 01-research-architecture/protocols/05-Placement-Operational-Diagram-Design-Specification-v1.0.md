# Placement Operational Diagram Design Specification v1.0

## Status

Operational Diagram Design

Version 1.0

---

## Purpose

This document defines the design specification for the Placement Operational Diagram.

The objective is not to explain the Placement Principle.

Instead, the diagram provides a concise visual representation of the operational placement procedure defined by Placement Protocol v1.0.

The diagram functions as a reusable operational companion to the protocol document.

---

## Design Objectives

The diagram should:

- visualize the complete operational placement procedure,
- emphasize the sequential operational flow,
- preserve procedural clarity,
- remain easy to understand at a glance,
- support long-term repository operations.

---

## Diagram Type

Operational Protocol Diagram

The diagram represents a standardized operational procedure rather than a conceptual framework or workflow.

---

## Central Operational Flow

```text
Asset Identification
        │
        ▼
Role Identification
        │
        ▼
Placement Evaluation
        │
        ▼
Repository Placement
        │
        ▼
Consistency Verification
        │
        ▼
Placement Finalization
```

---

## Visual Design Policy

The diagram should:

- use a single vertical flow,
- avoid decision branches,
- avoid explanatory paragraphs,
- emphasize procedural progression,
- maintain visual simplicity,
- prioritize operational readability over graphical complexity.

---

## Visual Elements

Include:

- title,
- six operational steps,
- directional arrows,
- consistent spacing,
- simple framing if appropriate.

Do not include:

- methodological explanations,
- repository examples,
- detailed annotations,
- implementation notes.

---

## Intended Audience

The diagram is intended for:

- repository maintainers,
- research program contributors,
- future operational users of the Placement Protocol.

---

## Relationship to Related Assets

This diagram accompanies:

- Placement Protocol v1.0
- Placement Design Memo v1.0

Together these documents form the operational package supporting consistent repository placement.

---

## Version 1.0 Policy

Version 1.0 intentionally prioritizes operational clarity.

Future versions may introduce additional visual refinements without changing the underlying operational sequence.

---
