# Operational Protocols

## Purpose

This directory contains reusable operational protocols developed within the AI Text Project.

Unlike methodological documents, which explain conceptual foundations and observational principles, the documents stored here define standardized operational procedures intended for repeated practical use.

The purpose of this directory is to preserve operational consistency, reproducibility, and long-term maintainability across research activities.

---

# Relationship to Research Architecture

The protocols contained in this directory are derived from previously established methodological frameworks.

Their role is not to introduce new methodology.

Instead, they operationalize existing methodological knowledge into executable procedures.

The overall relationship is:

```text
Methodology
        │
        ▼
Workflow
        │
        ▼
Operational Protocol
        │
        ▼
Operational Execution
```

# Design Principles

Protocols stored in this directory should:

- define standardized operational procedures,
- remain independent from extensive methodological discussion,
- maximize reproducibility,
- preserve operational clarity,
- support long-term maintenance.

Whenever possible, design rationale should be documented separately from the operational procedure itself.

---

# Relationship to Design Documentation

Each protocol may be accompanied by a corresponding Design Memo.

The protocol specifies:

> **How should the procedure be executed?**

The Design Memo explains:

> **Why was the procedure designed this way?**

Maintaining this separation improves both operational usability and future revision.
