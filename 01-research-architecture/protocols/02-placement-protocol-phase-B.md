## Phase B — Operational Workflow

### Step 1 — Asset Identification

#### Objective

Identify the research asset that requires repository placement.

---

#### Input

- Research asset
- Supporting documents (if available)

---

#### Procedure

1. Identify the asset requiring placement.
2. Confirm that the asset has reached sufficient organizational stability.
3. Review the asset title and primary purpose.
4. Determine whether the asset represents a reusable organizational unit.
5. Prepare the asset for organizational evaluation.

---

#### Output

- Identified research asset
- Asset prepared for placement evaluation
