## Phase A — Foundation

### 1. Purpose

The purpose of this protocol is to provide a standardized operational procedure for placing research assets consistently within the repository.

Rather than introducing new organizational principles, the protocol translates the accumulated Placement Principle into a reusable operational procedure.

The protocol is intended to support long-term repository consistency, maintainability, and reproducible placement decisions.

---

### 2. Scope

This protocol applies to repository operations involving the placement of research assets, including:

- research architecture documents,
- methodological documents,
- research notes,
- Meta Cases,
- operational protocols,
- diagrams,
- cross-repository references,
- and other organizational assets requiring repository placement.

The protocol focuses on operational placement rather than methodological development.

---

### 3. Preconditions

Before applying this protocol, the following conditions should be satisfied:

- The asset has reached sufficient organizational stability.
- The organizational role of the asset can be identified.
- The repository structure is available for evaluation.
- Related documents and existing repository relationships can be reviewed if necessary.

The protocol assumes that placement decisions are based on accumulated organizational observations rather than individual preference.
