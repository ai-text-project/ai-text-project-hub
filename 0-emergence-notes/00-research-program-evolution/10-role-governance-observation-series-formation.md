# Role-Governance Observation Series Formation

## Status

Provisional Observation

---

## Purpose

To document the emergence of a new observation series concerning:

* role formation
* role specialization
* governance emergence
* role stabilization
* execution stability

within the broader evolution of the research program.

---

## Background

Earlier observations primarily focused on:

* dialogue processes
* structural recognition
* crystallization
* externalization
* research-space formation

As the research program expanded, multiple specialized operational roles emerged.

Examples included:

* Harvest Strategist
* Harvest Curator
* Research-to-Career Strategist
* Research Community Mapper
* Research Outreach Scout
* Operations Support

---

## Observed Development

Initially, role creation was viewed primarily as an operational necessity.

However, subsequent observations suggested that:

* role formation
* governance formation
* role-boundary recognition
* execution behavior

were themselves becoming observable phenomena.

This gradually shifted attention toward the relationship between:

Role

and

Governance

as potential observation targets.

Role-Governance Interaction
