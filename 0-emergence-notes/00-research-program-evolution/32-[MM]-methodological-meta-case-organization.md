## Purpose

This document records the current organizational structure emerging within the Methodological Meta Cases.

Its purpose is not to establish a finalized taxonomy.

Instead, it summarizes the present interpretation of how accumulated Methodological Meta Cases appear to organize themselves into distinct developmental groups.

The emphasis is placed on preserving the observed organizational structure rather than proposing a new theoretical framework.

---

# Background

Earlier Meta Cases primarily documented individual methodological observations.

As additional Methodological Meta Cases accumulated, recurring organizational tendencies gradually became visible.

Rather than representing unrelated methodological topics, recent Meta Cases increasingly appeared to describe complementary aspects of a common methodological organization.

The present document records the current working interpretation of that organization.

---

# Current Organizational Interpretation

Subsequent Methodological Meta Cases did not replace the Structural Observation Methodology established in Document 08. Instead, they progressively revealed its internal organizational structure through accumulated methodological observation.


Current observations provisionally suggest two major developmental series.

```text

├── Formation
│
│   Observation
│       ↓
│   Methodological Definition
│       ↓
│   Protocol
│       ↓
```

These two series should not be regarded as independent methodologies.

Instead, they appear to represent complementary developmental aspects of the Methodological Layer.

# Relationship Between the Two Series

Current observations suggest that the two developmental series are complementary.

```text
Formation

↓

Stable Methodological Layer

↓

Operation

↓

Methodological Refinement

↓

Formation
```

Methodological development therefore appears recursive rather than linear.

Conceptual formation supports methodological operation, while operational experience gradually refines conceptual methodology.

# Current Interpretation

The present organization is intentionally provisional.

The two-series interpretation is derived from currently accumulated observations.

Future Meta Cases may:

- refine existing groupings,
- reorganize current relationships,
- introduce additional methodological dimensions.

Accordingly, this document should be understood as a working organizational interpretation rather than a finalized classification system.
