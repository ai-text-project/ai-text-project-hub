## Purpose

This document records the historical assembly principle used to reconstruct the formation of the research program as a scholarly paper.

The purpose is **not** to introduce new theoretical concepts.

Instead, it provides a historical blueprint that aligns each chapter of the paper with a corresponding developmental event observed during the evolution of the research program.

---

## Historical Assembly Principle

The paper is treated as a **historical reconstruction** rather than a theoretical proposal.

Each chapter represents one major historical event.

Historical continuity therefore determines the paper structure.

The objective is to reconstruct the observed development of the research environment while minimizing retrospective reinterpretation.
