# Placement Principle Development Flow

## Purpose

This note provides a high-level overview of how the Placement Principle evolved from an emergent organizational observation into an operational repository guide.

The purpose is not to introduce new theoretical claims.

The purpose is to clarify the relationship between the principle-emergence documents and their later operational applications.

## Interpretation

The first sequence documents how the Placement Principle emerged.

The second sequence documents how the principle was subsequently applied within repository operations.

The Hub Repository therefore records the formation history of the principle.

The Dialogue Repository records its operational use.

---

## Structural Transition

The central transition may be summarized as:

```text
Placement Principle Emergence
            ↓
Placement Principle Stabilization
            ↓
Placement Principle Application
```

This transition reflects the movement from observation and interpretation toward operational implementation.
