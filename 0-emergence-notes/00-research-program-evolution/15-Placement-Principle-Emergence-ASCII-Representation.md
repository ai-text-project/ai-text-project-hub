## Interpretation

The diagram illustrates a provisional interpretation of how the Placement Principle emerged.

The process did not begin with a theoretical objective.

Instead, repeated placement difficulties led to repeated classification attempts.

These classification activities gradually shifted attention toward a deeper question:

> What is being observed?

Subsequent observations suggested that multiple dimensions were involved simultaneously, including:

* Structure
* Formation Processes
* Observation Processes
* Operational Processes

The Placement Principle emerged as a response to the need to organize these observations within a growing research space.
