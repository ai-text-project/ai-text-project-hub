# Research Space Topology Layer

This section documents the structural formation and evolution of the research space itself.

Unlike Repository Topology, which focuses on repositories and their relationships, this layer focuses on the emergence of a navigable and semantically organized research environment.

---

## Scope

Examples include:

- repository-to-research-space transition
- research-space formation
- research-space evolution
- research-space reconstruction
- adaptive restructuring
- semantic continuity across repositories
- topology-level organization

---

## Distinction

### Repository Topology

Focuses on:

- repository relations
- projection paths
- navigation structures
- repository organization

Primary question:

How are repositories connected?

---

### Research Space Topology

Focuses on:

- environment formation
- semantic integration
- structural continuity
- space-level organization

Primary question:

How does a research space emerge from repositories?
